About WeAreData Watchdog

Establishing digital trust by practicing what we preach. We audit our own data collection and user interaction guidelines.

WeAreData was founded to democratize technology ethics. Modern tech platforms hide invasive tracking, dopamine traps, and child exploitation policies behind thousands of words of dense legal print. We use state-of-the-art AI agents to scan these documents instantly and give you clear, actionable protection steps.

To build absolute trust with our community, we subject ourselves to the exact same ethical scrutiny we apply to others. Below is our official Self-Audit and Integrity Rating:

Data Ingestion & Saving

GREEN

Fully stateless. No data is stored, cached, or sold. No database footprints.

Read Self-Audit Findings

Self-Audit Justification

WeAreData runs entirely in memory. When you enter a service name or URL, the backend crawler retrieves the policy text ephemerally, parses it, runs the analysis, and delivers the results. Once the session ends, the memory is released. We store no history logs, IP records, or cookie tracking.

AI & Scraping Boundaries

While WeAreData uses Google Gemini to audit policies, users should be aware of key limits:

  • Model Hallucinations: Generative LLMs can occasionally misinterpret dense legal syntax or flag benign operational logs as high-risk trackers (false positives).
  • Policy Drift: Privacy terms change frequently. Our reports scan terms *at the moment of analysis*, but tech companies can alter policies in real time without notice.
  • Scraper Limits: head-less requests may be blocked by CAPTCHAs, leading to partial scans or offline database fallback summaries.
  • Offline Baselines (No User Sourcing): Our offline templates catalog is compiled from expert-curated public legal standards (like GDPR/CCPA template baselines). We never crowdsource, log, or harvest data from user scans to build this catalog, preventing unverified user-generated misinformation.
  • Not Legal Advice: This tool is built strictly for digital literacy and public transparency. It does not replace a compliance audit or formal legal counsel.
